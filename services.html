Interior Details

Maintain Your Shine In & Out Interior detailing involves a comprehensive cleaning and restoration process focused on the interior components of a vehicle. This meticulous procedure addresses areas such as seats, carpets, dashboard, and other surfaces to remove dirt, dust, and stains. Specialized tools and products are utilized to reach and clean hard-to-access nooks and crannies. Additionally, interior detailing extends to treatments like leather conditioning and fabric protection to maintain the integrity and aesthetic appeal of the vehicle's interior

Fabric Coating

Fabric coating is an essential component of interior detailing, designed to protect and preserve the upholstery of a vehicle. It forms a durable, invisible barrier on fabric surfaces, preventing liquids, dirt, and stains from penetrating the material. This hydrophobic shield makes spills bead up on the surface, allowing for easy cleanup and preventing permanent damage. Fabric coatings also often include UV inhibitors, which help prevent fading and degradation caused by sunlight exposure. By applying a fabric coating during detailing, you ensure a longer lifespan for your vehicle's upholstery, maintaining a clean and fresh interior appearance.

Ozone Machine

An ozone machine is a powerful tool used in interior detailing to eliminate odors and purify the air within a vehicle. It works by generating ozone, a highly reactive molecule, which effectively breaks down and neutralizes odorous molecules. This process is particularly useful for combating stubborn and deeply embedded smells such as cigarette smoke, pet odors, and mildew. Safety measures are crucial when using an ozone machine, as it's essential to ensure the vehicle is properly ventilated and free of occupants during the treatment.

Interior Ceramic Coating

Interior ceramic coating is a protective layer applied to the interior surfaces of a vehicle, such as leather, fabric, and plastic. It forms a durable barrier that guards against stains, spills, and UV damage, helping to maintain the pristine condition of the interior. This coating utilizes nanotechnology, allowing it to bond at a molecular level with the surfaces, creating a hydrophobic effect that repels liquids and makes cleaning easier. Additionally, it provides a subtle sheen, enhancing the aesthetic appeal of the interior while preserving its integrity over time.
